Gregorc Thinking Style

May 07, 2014

Professor Anthony Gregorc of the University of Connecticut describes concrete sequential thinkers (CS) and those that tend to be based in reality, processing information in an ordered, sequential, linear way. He describes abstract sequential thinkers (AS) as thos that love the wolrd of theory and abstract thought.
